This project focuses on developing an interactive **Tableau dashboard** to analyze and compare sales performance across two regions. The goal is to support leadership teams in making informed, data-driven decisions through clear and intuitive visual analytics.

Through this project, I gained hands-on experience in **sales analytics, business intelligence, and dashboard design**, strengthening my ability to transform raw datasets into meaningful insights that drive strategic action.

## üìù Business Scenario

The dashboard needed to provide visibility into:

## Project Workflow & Methodology

### 1.Concept Review & Planning
Before starting the analysis, I revisited key Tableau and data visualization concepts, including:

- Data preparation and cleaning techniques  
- Calculated fields and aggregations  
- Effective chart selection  
- Dashboard layout and design principles  
- Filters, parameters, and interactive features  
- Best practices for data storytelling  

This step ensured a structured and efficient project execution.

---

### 2.Data Exploration & Preparation
The dataset was examined to understand its structure, variables, and quality. This included:

- Reviewing regional, sales, product, and date-related fields  
- Identifying missing values or inconsistencies  
- Preparing the data for analysis and visualization  
- Ensuring accurate aggregation and time-based analysis  

Clean and well-structured data was critical to producing reliable insights.

---

### 3.Regional Sales Analysis
Using Tableau, I conducted a detailed analysis by:

- Filtering the dataset to focus on the two selected regions  
- Calculating KPIs such as total sales, average sales, and growth metrics  
- Analyzing sales performance across different time periods  
- Comparing product categories and subcategories  
- Identifying trends, seasonal patterns, and performance gaps  

This analysis helped uncover meaningful differences and similarities between the regions.

---

### 4.Dashboard Design & Development
An interactive Tableau dashboard was created with a clear and logical layout, featuring:

- Line charts to display monthly or quarterly sales trends  
- Bar charts for category and subcategory comparisons  
- KPI summary sections for quick performance evaluation  
- Interactive filters for region, product category, and date  
- A user-friendly flow from high-level insights to detailed analysis  

The dashboard design prioritized clarity, usability, and decision support.

---

### 5.Insight Interpretation & Storytelling
A narrative was developed to explain the analytical findings, focusing on:

- Key performance differences between regions  
- Factors contributing to strong sales outcomes  
- Areas where performance improvement is needed  
- Practical recommendations for business leaders  
- How insights can be applied to strategic planning  

This ensured the dashboard was not just visual, but also actionable.
